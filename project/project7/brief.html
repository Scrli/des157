Project Brief

Topic summary

The topic of my project is a food delivery and food community app specifically for Chinese international students. According to my research, influent language, an unfamiliar lifestyle, no family nearby and expensive food are barriers to international Chinese students’ adaptation to college life in the U.S. They have the same pain point: loneliness in a totally new environment, and different food preferrence. Food is also a heated topic for them to communicate with friends. Thus, a food community and food delivery app can both satisfy their social needs and necessary needs of food. 

Target audience description

The target audience of the project is Chinese International students oversea. 31.5% of the 886,000 international students at American college in 2013 and 2014 came from China. They are from 17 to 25 year old. They gain high education abroad from undergrad to Phd. They value good UIUX, high quality photos as well as good delivery service.

The goals of the project

The goal of the project is to make an actual app for Chinese international students at UCD use in this year. Students can order Chinese hometown flavor food through the app and get the food delivered in 20 mins. They can also make friends with the same interest towards food in the app.

The goals for the overall look and feel

The goals for the overall look should be pretty neat, and user-friendly. The basic color should be white to emphasize on the dishes. The menu will include high quality images, which provide better detailed info to the users.
