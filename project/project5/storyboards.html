Storyboards

Setting:
  • International Chinese Students, delivery men, and restuarants bosses are target audiences.
  • When students do not want and have no time to cook, they can order the food by the app online and get food deliveried by delivery man from restuarants.
  • When students want to share good food or food moments with friends, they can upload the moment online and share with friends in the community in the app.
Sequence:
  • To order the food to be deliveried, students need to use online manu in the app, then order and pay online.
  • To deliver the food, the delivery men need to use the app to navigate to the location of students, and access their contact info.
  • To prepare food from orders, the restuarants need to use the app to get online orders and students' info automatically.
Satisfaction
  • The app have one sentense purpose:
    "One click makes Chinese International students get hometown flavor food deliveried in 15 mins."
